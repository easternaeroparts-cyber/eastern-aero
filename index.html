About Eastern Aero

Aviation Expertise You Can Rely On

Eastern Aero is a globally recognised aviation parts and services provider, specialising in turboprop and regional jet platforms. We combine an extensive inventory, a worldwide supplier network, and deep technical knowledge to keep your fleet airworthy — on time and within budget.

  • Global Supplier Network — Access to thousands of certified OEM, serviceable, and overhauled parts from trusted suppliers on every continent.
  • Flexible Financing Options — Exchange, loan, and outright purchase programs designed to reduce your capital investment and downtime.
  • AOG Emergency Response — Round-the-clock support ensuring critical components reach your team within hours, not days.
  • Documentation Compliance — All parts supplied with full traceability, FAA 8130-3, EASA Form 1, or equivalent release documents.
